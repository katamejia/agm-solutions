SMS Consent & Messaging Disclosure

AGMBS Shop Management

By providing your mobile phone number to AGMBS Shop Management, you consent to receive SMS messages related to automotive services you have requested.

Message Types

  • Service quotes
  • Repair status updates
  • Appointment reminders
  • Vehicle ready notifications

How Users Opt In

Customers provide consent in person at our automotive workshop during the vehicle intake process. Our service advisors verbally ask customers if they would like to receive service-related SMS notifications. Consent is recorded in our internal management system.

Message Frequency

Message frequency may vary depending on the service activity.

Opt-Out Instructions

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.

For help, reply HELP or contact us at support@agmbs.com.
